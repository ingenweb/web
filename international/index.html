The Ingenes Difference

What no other clinic in the world offers

Most fertility clinics offer one thing: Assisted Reproduction. Ingenes integrates three medical disciplines simultaneously to give you the best possible outcome.

🔬

Reproductive Medicine

IVF, ICSI, IUI, egg freezing, egg and sperm donation, surrogacy, multi-cycle programs with money-back guarantee. The full spectrum of assisted reproduction, from low to high complexity.

🧬

Regenerative Medicine & Genetics

Ovagen® regenerates ovarian tissue. Endogen® restores damaged endometrium. IVF MORE® restores eggs at the mitochondrial level. PGT-A and PGT-M to transfer only genetically healthy embryos. Sex selection, legal in Mexico. Exclusive technologies developed by our research team.

⚡

Metabolic Medicine

We assess and optimize how your body processes energy: nutrition, hormonal profile, inflammation, insulin resistance. A metabolically prepared body responds better to every treatment. No more “blind IVF.”

How It Works

Two paths. One world-class experience.

Whether you start through one of our U.S. locations or go directly to Mexico, you’ll always travel to Mexico for your procedure. Our labs are in Mexico — that’s one of the reasons we can offer world-class care at significantly lower costs, and we pass that benefit directly to you.

🇺🇸

Through a U.S. location

Orange County, CA  •  Houston, TX

  • U.S.-licensed physician manages your case
  • Prescriptions for medications filled in the U.S.
  • Initial workup and follow-up at your U.S. location
  • Financing options available (Future Family, PatientFi, CapexMD)
  • Travel to Mexico only for the procedure (typically 7 days)
  • Dedicated English-speaking coordinator throughout
🇲🇽

Directly in Mexico

20 locations across Mexico

  • Virtual consultations available before traveling
  • You'll only be traveling to Mexico for your treatment (typically 7 days)
  • Lowest possible costs — no intermediary
  • Access to all 11 labs nationwide
  • Dedicated English-speaking coordinator throughout
  • Post-treatment follow-up via telemedicine
✈️
In both cases, you travel to Mexico for the procedure. Our internationally certified labs are in Mexico. That’s how we maintain world-class standards at costs significantly lower than in the U.S. — and we pass that benefit directly to our patients.
